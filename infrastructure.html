Infrastructure FAQ

Why should I own my infrastructure?

Shared providers (like Gmail or Outlook) can ban you instantly for cold outreach. When you own the infra, you control the IP reputation, the rules, and you pay $0 in recurring license fees.

What does "Warmed Senders" mean?

New domains are often blocked by spam filters. We "warm" them up by sending human-like traffic for weeks before delivery, so your reputation is already solid on Day 1.

Is the 90% payment truly after delivery?

Yes. We are so confident in our setup that we only take a 10% commitment fee to cover initial domain/server costs. You pay the rest once the dashboard and 50 mailboxes are live and working.

What happens after 1 year?

You can either take over the server billing directly (~$10-20/mo) or we can continue managing it for a small monthly maintenance fee. No lock-ins.
