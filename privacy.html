Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 15, 2026

1. Data Ownership

Cleanmails is designed to be 100% private. Since the script is self-hosted on your machine or server, we never see, touch, or store the email addresses you validate. Your data stays entirely under your control.

2. Information We Collect

We only collect information necessary to process your purchase and provide support:

  • Email address (for delivery and support)
  • Billing information (processed securely via Gumroad)
  • Support communications

3. Third-Party Services

The Cleanmails script itself does not communicate with any third-party APIs. It performs validations locally. Our website uses Gumroad for payment processing, which has its own privacy policy.

4. Security

Because you host the script yourself, the security of the validation process depends on your own server configuration. We recommend following security best practices for your hosting environment.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding your privacy while using Cleanmails, please reach out via our support page or email us directly at hello@cleanmails.online.
